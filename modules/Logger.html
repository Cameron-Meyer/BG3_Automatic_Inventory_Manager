Module Logger

Third party mods are encouraged to handle their own logging, for better traceability, but exposing this for my own convenience.

It's HIGHLY recommended to keep the logging to a minimum, especially logs that are created per FilterProcessor iteration, as writing logs to the log file is extremely performance intensive. May look into refactoring in the future, but for now, users can just set their log levels appropriately.
